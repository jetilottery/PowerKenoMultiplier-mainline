Starship Keno Multiplier

Explore interstellar riches in Starship Keno Multiplier! Select up to 10 spots on the keno board. When the game starts, 6 multiplier spots also appear on the board. The cross-hairs select 20 keno numbers. If numbers match, you win a prize based on the prize table! And if 2 or more multiplier spots match, that prize is multiplied – up to 10x!

How to Bet

Spots

Displays the current number of spots to select.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the number of spots to select.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the number of spots to select.

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

The object of Starship Keno Multiplier is to match keno numbers to the player’s selected numbers or multiplier numbers.

Select number spots on the board or press QUICK PICK to select numbers automatically. Press Go. Multiplier spots randomly appear on the board. After all selections are made, 20 keno numbers are drawn and the cross-hairs travel over the board indicating the drawn numbers.

Match keno numbers to the drawn numbers to win based on the prize table. When 2 or more multiplier spots match, the prize is multiplied by the multiplier value.

Play ends when all drawn numbers have been indicated on the board. A message appears indicating the game’s outcome.

Speed

Press SPEED to change the keno numbers drawing speed. There are 3 speed options.

Quick Pick

Press QUICK PICK to automatically select numbers. Game play continues until all selections have been made.

Clear

Press CLEAR to clear all the selections.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Wins are shown in currency.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game’s outcome.
